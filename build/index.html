Studio123 is a wonder factory.

We create the customer experience — from the brand they love, to the marketing pieces they touch, to the digital world where they interact. We have the toolbox to tackle all your creative needs.

Take your work seriously, not yourself.

We believe in working hard, playing hard, and often blending the two. Einstein said that "Play is the highest form of research." Play sparks exploration. Play keeps us from getting bored and boring. Play is good for business.

Good design is good for business.

Good design is essential to overcome today’s challenges. We organize complex ideas into simple, beautiful tools that work for you. We will help you craft your communications to help grow your business and reach your goals.

Collaboration is the common thread.

We believe that the best things stem from collaboration. Studio123's approach fosters collaboration between our team and our yours and drives everything we create. Even our physical studio space is conducive to team-oriented creativity and design. Collaboration means that we succeed when you succeed.

Know the rules. Have fun breaking them.

In order for us to do our best work, we have set up some boundaries. Since we do our best creative work in the mornings, we only schedule meetings in the afternoons and and do our best to avoid email and other things electronic before 10 AM.
